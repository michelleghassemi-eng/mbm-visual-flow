Notes / Notas:

Time averages from measured worker data (n=39 batches, 4 workers). RC team avg = 7.6 sec/oz. Cut team avg = 4.6 sec/oz. Avg batch = 286oz.

RC bottleneck: max 10 machines/day at current speeds. 11+ machines requires 2nd Robot Coupe.

Machine unload times: Medium 39 min · Large 36 min · XL 46 min · Mega 59 min (full cycle including defrost excluded — runs unattended).

⏱ = fixed duration regardless of volume  ·  ⚖ = scales with oz being processed
